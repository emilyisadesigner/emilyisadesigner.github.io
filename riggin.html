Website Design & Branding

The Schooner J. & E. Riggin is an almost hundred year old oyster boat that has been retrofitted over the years to become an all-inclusive sailing experience on Penobscot Bay.

Now under new ownership, I updated their branding and website to reflect the history and future of the ship.

Sailing aboard one of these historical vessels is unlike any other vacation or “cruise” most people have heard of. I worked closely with the business owners to communicate the unique experience of this all-inclusive adventure vacation, with a special emphasis on high-quality photography to show, rather than explain, the beauty and difference of these trips.
